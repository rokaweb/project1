Maggi Milkshake

Creamy, savory, and oddly comforting, spiced Maggi blended into a smooth, warm milkshake that defies logic but seduces curiosity.

Description

Ingredients:

  • Cooked Maggi noodles (without excess water)
  • Milk (warm)
  • Butter
  • Maggi tastemaker
  • Black pepper (optional)

Steps:

  1. Cook Maggi normally but slightly over-soften it.
  2. Add noodles, milk, butter, and tastemaker to a blender.
  3. Blend until smooth and frothy.
  4. Heat gently if needed and add pepper.
  5. Serve warm in a large glass and enjoy.
