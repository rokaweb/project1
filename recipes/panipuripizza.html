Pani Puri Pizza

A crisp pizza base crowned with spicy mashed potatoes, tangy chutneys, and crunchy puris delivers a street-food explosion in every bite.

Description

Ingredients:

  • Pizza base
  • Boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • Green chutney
  • Tamarind chutney
  • Onion (finely chopped)
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pani Puri shells
  • Chaat masala

Steps:

  1. Spread green and tamarind chutney on the pizza base.
  2. Add a layer of mashed potato and onions.
  3. Sprinkle cheese generously.
  4. Bake until cheese melts and base is crisp.
  5. Top with puris and chaat masala before serving.
