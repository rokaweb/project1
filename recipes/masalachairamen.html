Gulab Jamun Grilled Cheese

Silky noodles swim in a fragrant chai-spiced broth where cardamom, ginger, and cream wrap the palate in warm, aromatic comfort.

Description

Ingredients:

  • Ramen noodles
  • Water + milk
  • Black tea leaves
  • Ginger
  • Cardamom, cinnamon, cloves
  • Sugar (light)
  • Chili oil (optional)

Steps:

  1. Boil water with ginger and whole spices.
  2. Add tea leaves, then milk and strain.
  3. Simmer ramen noodles directly in the chai broth.
  4. Balance with a touch of sugar and salt.
  5. Finish with chili oil for contrast and serve hot.
