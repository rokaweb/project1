Gulab Jamun Grilled Cheese

Golden, buttery bread embraces molten cheese and syrup-soaked gulab jamun, where sweet meets savory in a rich, indulgent melt.

Description

Ingredients:

  • Gulab jamun (slightly crushed)
  • Sliced bread (thick)
  • Mozzarella or mild cheddar cheese
  • Butter
  • Cardamom powder (pinch)

Steps:

  1. Lightly mash gulab jamun and mix with a pinch of cardamom.
  2. Butter both sides of the bread
  3. Layer cheese, gulab jamun mixture, then more cheese between slices.
  4. Grill on low heat until bread is crisp and cheese is fully melted.
  5. Rest for 30 seconds, slice, and serve warm with a bowl of honey for extra fun.
