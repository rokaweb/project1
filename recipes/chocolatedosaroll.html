Chocolate Dosa Roll

A paper-thin crispy dosa rolled with molten chocolate and roasted peanuts delivers crunch, sweetness, and nostalgia in harmony.

Description

Ingredients:

  • Dosa batter
  • Dark or milk chocolate
  • Roasted peanuts (crushed)
  • Butter or ghee

Steps:

  1. Spread dosa batter thin on a hot pan and drizzle ghee.
  2. Cook until crisp but flexible.
  3. Sprinkle chopped chocolate and peanuts evenly.
  4. Let chocolate melt slightly, then roll tightly.
  5. Serve hot for maximum crunch.
